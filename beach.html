Beaches and Resorts

Discover Argao's pristine beaches and welcoming resorts, where powder‑soft sands, clear turquoise waters, and swaying coconut palms invite relaxation and adventure. From snorkeling vibrant reefs to enjoying warm local hospitality, Argao offers unforgettable seaside escapes for families, couples, and solo travelers alike.

Bulasa Mangroves

Bulasa Mangroves is an eco-friendly resort in Barangay Bulasa, Argao, Cebu. It’s more than just a place to stay — it blends natural mangrove ecosystems, beach features, and resort comforts.
