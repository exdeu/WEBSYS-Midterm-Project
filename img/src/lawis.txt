🏄‍♂️🏄‍♀️ARGAO - Siargao of Southern Cebu🌊
Argao is considered the 'Siargao of Southern Cebu', particularly Lawis Point, which is a favorite spot for skimboarding and surfing. Lawis Point offers excellent conditions for riding waves, with strong winds pushing riders to their limits.
This location is ideal for board sports, boasting an extended beach area that stretches along Argao's coastline. Beyond surfing and skimboarding, Lawis Point is also popular for various beach activities, including beach volleyball, sunbathing, and picnicking. Regardless of the wind direction, Lawis Point consistently provides good waves from both the north and south.
