Explore Argao

Experience the beauty and culture of Argao, Cebu. Discover stunning beaches, historical landmarks, and vibrant festivals that make this town a must-visit destination.

Beaches and Resorts

Discover Argao's pristine beaches, from Taloot to Mahayahay, offering swimming, snorkeling, and sunbathing. Local resorts provide comfortable stays with modern amenities while maintaining authentic Cebuano hospitality, perfect for both adventure and relaxation.

Springs

Argao's natural springs are clear, cool pools in limestone hills, perfect for swimming and picnicking. Locally maintained trails and community conservation keep these peaceful sites clean and ecologically vibrant.

Events

Argao's lively festivals include the Pitlagong in September, the La Torta in October, and the Feast of St. Michael (Sept 29), featuring processions, street dancing, and local performances.

Historical Sites

Argao's historical sites reflect its Spanish colonial past, from the San Miguel Arcángel Church and stone watchtowers to old bridges and ancestral houses. They showcase the town's religious and civic heritage.
