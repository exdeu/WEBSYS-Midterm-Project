Events

Experience Argao’s vibrant events, from colorful religious festivals and lively beach gatherings to traditional music, food fairs, and cultural performances that celebrate the town’s heritage and community spirit.

Bangkarera

Bangkarera, held each September during the La Torta Festival, features lively coastal boat races that celebrate Argao’s seafaring heritage and community spirit.

Learn more Argao, Cebu
Miss Argao Competition

Miss Argao, held during the La Torta Festival, is a pageant showcasing the beauty, talent, and cultural ambassadorship of young Argawanon women.

Learn more Argao, Cebu
Torta Eating Contest

The Torta Eating Contest, held during the La Torta Festival, challenges contestants to eat as many Argao tortas as possible and celebrates the town’s famous delicacy and community spirit.

Learn more Argao, Cebu
Pitgalong Festival

The Pitlagong Festival honors Argao’s patron saint, Michael the Archangel, featuring a parade, street dancing, and a ritual showdown. The pitlagong, a bamboo instrument, is essential for cleaning the sugong, a container for collecting tuba, a traditional Filipino drink.

Learn more Argao, Cebu
La Torta Festival

The La Torta Festival, held every September in Argao, Cebu, honors St. Michael the Archangel and celebrates the town’s famous torta, a sweet sponge cake made with tuba. The event features colorful parades, cultural dances, and fun competitions that showcase Argao’s rich heritage and community spirit.

Learn more Argao, Cebu
Argao Skimboarding Competition

The Argao Skimboarding Competition is an exciting event that attracts local surfers and beach lovers. Participants demonstrate their skills on skimboards, showcasing agility and balance while promoting Argao’s coastal lifestyle and tourism.

Learn more Argao, Cebu
