Springs

Discover the hidden paradise of Argao’s natural springs, where crystal-clear waters, lush greenery, and the soothing sounds of nature create the perfect escape. Whether you’re looking to unwind, swim, or simply breathe in the fresh mountain air, Argao’s springs promise a refreshing adventure you’ll never forget.

Bulasa Linaw

Bulasa Spring, or Linaw Spring, in Argao, Cebu, is known for its clear waters and peaceful setting, making it a favorite local spot to relax and swim.

Tubod/Lalaan Spring

Lalaan Spring in Argao, Cebu, is a peaceful cold spring with clear waters and lush surroundings, offering a refreshing spot to swim and relax in nature.

Learn more Argao, Cebu
Liki Spring

Liki Spring in Taloot, Argao, Cebu, is a natural cold spring with clear water flowing from a rock crack, offering a peaceful and refreshing spot to swim and relax.

Kaibo Spring Resort

Kaibo Spring is a serene natural spot with clear waters and lush surroundings, offering a refreshing place to swim and unwind in nature.

Kilat Spring Resort

Kilat Spring and Resort is a tranquil nature getaway with a cool, spring-fed pool surrounded by greenery, offering a refreshing spot to swim and relax in a peaceful, eco-friendly setting.

Ka Treasure Water Terraces

Ka Treasure Water Terraces in Argao features beautiful tiered pools of clear spring water surrounded by greenery and limestone, offering a refreshing and scenic spot to relax and enjoy nature.
